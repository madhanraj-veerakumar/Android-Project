Project Overview
Expense Tracker is an Android application that allows users to track their personal expenses. The app enables users to input their daily expenses, categorize them, and view their spending patterns over time. The goal of this application is to help users manage their finances by offering an intuitive and simple interface to record and visualize their expenses.

Features
Add Items Expenses: Users can add expenses of Each Item they Buy
View Expense Summary: Provides users with a summary of their spending, including total expenses and list of Items.
Date Filtering: Allows users to view expenses from specific time periods (e.g., daily, weekly, monthly).(Still in Update)
Expense History: Users can access a list of all their past expenses, including amounts, and dates.
